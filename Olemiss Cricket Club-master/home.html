OMCC Ole Miss Cricket Club (OMCC) was officially launched during the summer of 2006 primarily due to the efforts of Mr. Pramod Patlolla, Dr. Amar Chittiboyina and Dr. Herath Bandara. Immediately following the launch of the club, we had a league game with three University teams and an affiliate team from nearby companies. We are always looking for cricket enthusiasts who would either like to join us or invite our team to play professional cricket games.
